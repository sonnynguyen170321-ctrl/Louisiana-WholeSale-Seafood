Common Questions

Wholesale FAQs

Everything you need to know before placing your first order.

Minimums vary by species. Crawfish starts at one 30 lb sack. Blue crab is sold by the bushel. Shrimp is available in 10 lb increments. Contact us at (337) 453-7348 for specific minimums by product.
We primarily serve the Dallas–Fort Worth Metroplex, including Plano, Frisco, McKinney, Arlington, and surrounding areas. For locations outside DFW, contact us to check availability — we work with third-party carriers for extended routes.
We recommend ordering 24–48 hours in advance for standard products. For live crawfish and live blue crab, place orders by Thursday for Friday–weekend fulfillment. Rush orders may be accommodated depending on current dock inventory.
Both. We supply live crawfish, live blue crab, and fresh-off-the-boat shrimp in season. We also carry IQF (individually quick frozen) shrimp, frozen crab meat, and processed fillets for year-round kitchen planning.
New accounts are COD (cash or card on delivery). Established accounts with good history may qualify for net-15 terms. We accept cash, card, and ACH transfer. Reach us at louisianawholesaleseafood@gmail.com to discuss terms.
The vast majority of our product is wild-caught from the Gulf Coast — crawfish from the Atchafalaya Basin, shrimp from Barataria Bay, crabs from Lake Pontchartrain, and oysters from Caminada Bay. We'll always specify the source and harvest method on your quote sheet.
