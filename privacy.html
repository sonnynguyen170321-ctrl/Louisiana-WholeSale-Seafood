Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 13, 2026

Louisiana Wholesale Seafood ("we," "us," or "our") operates this website and the Fat Oysters Seafood restaurant. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information you provide when you visit our website, submit a wholesale inquiry, or sign up for SMS alerts.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you voluntarily provide through our website forms:

  • Wholesale Inquiry Form: Contact name, business name, email address, phone number, product interest, order volume, and delivery preferences.
  • SMS Alert Signup: First name and mobile phone number.

We do not automatically collect cookies, analytics data, or tracking pixels beyond what your browser sends as standard HTTP headers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to wholesale partnership and sourcing inquiries.
  • To send you SMS notifications about fresh catch arrivals and shipment updates (SMS subscribers only).
  • To contact you regarding your reservation or dinner party request.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. SMS Messaging

By subscribing to our SMS alert program, you consent to receive text messages regarding live seafood catch arrivals and availability updates. Message frequency varies. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any message.

4. Data Security

Your form submissions are processed securely through Netlify Forms. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access. However, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Links

Our website links to external sites (our online shop at lcwholesaleseafood.com and social media platforms). We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
