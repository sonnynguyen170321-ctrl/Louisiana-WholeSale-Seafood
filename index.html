Our Heritage

Dock-to-Door Sourcing Authenticity

Louisiana Wholesale Seafood was founded with a single mission: to eliminate the complex middle-layers of seafood distribution that compromise freshness. We establish direct partnerships with local captains and crawfish farmers along the Louisiana coast.

Every morning, our transport fleet departs Louisiana with live catches kept in custom temperature-controlled systems. Within hours, the haul arrives in North Texas, delivering restaurant-ready seafood that is unmatched in flavor, texture, and quality.
